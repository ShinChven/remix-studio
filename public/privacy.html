Privacy Policy

Remix Studio · Last updated: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Remix Studio ("we", "us", "the Service") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the application, including when you connect third-party social media accounts such as Threads, X (Twitter), and Google.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Your email address and authentication credentials (passwords are stored only as salted hashes; passkeys/WebAuthn credentials where used).
  • Profile and usage data you create within the Service (libraries, projects, prompts, generated media, campaigns, and posts).

Connected social accounts

When you connect a social platform, we receive and store only what is needed to publish on your behalf:

  • Threads / Meta: your Threads account ID, profile name, and avatar, plus OAuth access and refresh tokens scoped to threads_basic and threads_content_publish.
  • X (Twitter): your account ID, profile details, and OAuth tokens needed to publish posts.
  • Google: basic profile and email used for sign-in and invite-based registration.

Access and refresh tokens are encrypted at rest. We do not read your private messages, and we only access the permissions (scopes) you explicitly grant during authorization.

Technical data

  • Standard server logs (such as timestamps and request metadata) used to operate and secure the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core functionality: creating, managing, and scheduling content.
  • To publish posts to the social accounts you connect, at your direction.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To maintain, debug, and improve the Service.

We do not sell your personal data or your social account data to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • The social platforms you connect (e.g., Meta/Threads, X), strictly to publish the content you ask us to publish.
  • Infrastructure providers used to host the Service and store media (databases and S3-compatible object storage).
  • Authorities, where required by law.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can remove data at any time:

  • Disconnect a social account in Campaigns → Channels. This immediately deletes the stored account record and its OAuth tokens from the Service.
  • Uninstall / data-deletion via Meta: when you remove the app from your Threads/Meta settings, Meta notifies the Service and we automatically delete the associated Threads account record and tokens.
  • Full account deletion: contact us using the details below to request deletion of your account and associated data.

Requesting deletion of your Threads data

To delete the data associated with your Threads connection, disconnect the channel in Campaigns → Channels, or remove Remix Studio from your Threads app settings. Either action permanently deletes your stored Threads account ID and access tokens from our systems. For any other data-deletion request, email us at the address below.

5. Security

We protect your data with industry-standard measures, including encryption of sensitive credentials and access tokens at rest, hashed passwords, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information and promptly address any issues.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us below.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at: shinchven@gmail.com.
